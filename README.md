**ArtisanConnectAI** is a web-based e-commerce platform designed to support local artisans by enabling them to sell their eco-friendly and handcrafted products directly to consumers. The platform removes the dependency on intermediaries and leverages AI to simplify product onboarding through multilingual voice-to-text product description generation.

## âœ… Core Features

###  AI-Assisted Product Listing
- Artisans can upload product details via **voice recording in their preferred language**.
- The system converts voice input to text and **auto-generates product descriptions**.

###  Direct Artisan-to-Customer Model
- Eliminates middlemen to ensure artisans receive **fair and maximum profit margins**.
- Builds trust and transparency between artisans and customers.

###  Sustainable Product Marketplace
- Focused on eco-friendly, ethical, and handcrafted goods.
- Encourages conscious consumption and environmental responsibility.

###  Seamless User Experience
- Clean and intuitive UI for both artisans and customers.
- Designed with accessibility and simplicity as key principles.
